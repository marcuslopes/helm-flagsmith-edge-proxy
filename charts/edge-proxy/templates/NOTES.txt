Edge Proxy is installed. Config is loaded from the Secret "{{ .Values.existingSecret }}".

To retrieve feature flags from the host:
  1. Port-forward: kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "edge-proxy.fullname" . }} 8000:8000
  2. Get flags: curl -H "X-Environment-Key: YOUR_CLIENT_SIDE_KEY" http://localhost:8000/api/v1/flags/
  3. Or identity flags: curl -H "X-Environment-Key: YOUR_CLIENT_SIDE_KEY" "http://localhost:8000/api/v1/identities/?identifier=user123"

Ensure the Secret contains API_URL and ENVIRONMENT_KEY_PAIRS (see deploy/edge-proxy-secret.example.yaml).
